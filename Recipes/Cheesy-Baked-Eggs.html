Cheesy baked eggs

Recipe description

This basically is eggs, on top of cheese, on top of cheese, on top of eggs – making a delicious concoction of eggs, cheese, and flour.

To add a little extra kick, add a small can of diced chiles or jalapenos into the mix. Serve with sour cream, salsa, and green taco sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 12 eggs, beaten
  • 1 pound shredded pepper Jack cheese
  • 1 (16 ounce) container cottage cheese
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Prepare a 9x13-inch baking dish with melted butter.

  2. Whisk together the eggs, pepper Jack cheese, cottage cheese, flour, and salt in a large bowl.

  3. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until eggs are completely set, about 1 hour.

  5. Enjoy your meal!
